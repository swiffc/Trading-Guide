# 📊 Trading Patterns & Setups Legend

## Pattern Types

### 🔴 M-Top Patterns (Bearish)
- **TYPE 1:** Safety Trade - Asian Range violation
- **TYPE 2:** Asian 00 Bounce - Price bounces at .00 level
- **TYPE 3:** Asian 50 Bounce - Price bounces at .50 level
- **TYPE 4:** Breakout Continuation - Break and retest

### 🟢 W-Bottom Patterns (Bullish)
- **TYPE 1:** Safety Trade - Asian Range violation
- **TYPE 2:** Asian 00 Bounce - Price bounces at .00 level
- **TYPE 3:** Asian 50 Bounce - Price bounces at .50 level
- **TYPE 4:** Breakout Continuation - Break and retest

### 👤 Head & Shoulders
- Classic reversal pattern
- Left shoulder, head, right shoulder formation
- Neckline break = entry signal

### 🎯 Triple Hits
- Three touches of same level
- Third touch = highest probability entry
- Works on support/resistance zones

### 🦇 Half Batman (Half Bat)
- Harmonic pattern
- 50% retracement entry
- High win rate at key levels

## Setup Types

### ⏰ 1H 50-50 Bounce (TYPE 4d Variation)
- **Classification:** TYPE 4: Breakout Continuation
- **Timeframe:** 1-Hour chart (CRITICAL - must be 1H!)
- **Entry:** Price bounces at .50 level (e.g., 1.0750, 1.1050)
- **Setup:** Wait for rejection candle at .50 after breakout
- **Stop:** 15-20 pips below/above .50 level
- **Target:** Next .00 level (50 pips) or Large Quarter (250 pips)
- **Why TYPE 4:** Breakout → Retracement to .50 → Continuation

### 📦 Box Trades (12, 21, 22)
- **12 Box:** Daily high to Asian high (accumulation)
- **21 Box:** Asian high to London high (manipulation)
- **22 Box:** Consolidation range (distribution setup)
- **Entry:** Break and retest of box edges
- **Stop:** Outside opposite box edge
- **Target:** Box height measured move

### 🎯 ID 50 Only
- **Setup:** Institutional Detection at .50 levels
- **Entry:** Large institutional candle at .50 level
- **Confirmation:** Volume spike + body close strength
- **Stop:** Beyond institutional candle wick
- **Target:** Next quarter point (.00 or .75)

## Integration with Your System

### How Patterns Fit into Quarterly Theory

**Weekly Cycle:**
- **Monday (Q1):** Mark levels, watch for box formation
- **Tuesday (Q2):** Look for M-Top TYPE 1 (if bearish week)
- **Wednesday (Q3):** BEST - TYPE 1 + 1H 50-50 Bounce + Triple Hit
- **Thursday (Q4):** TYPE 4 continuations or reversals
- **Friday:** Take profits, avoid new trades

**Daily Cycle:**
- **Asian (Q1):** Mark range, identify .00 and .50 levels
- **London (Q2):** Watch for box trades (12, 21, 22)
- **NY AM (Q3):** ENTER - TYPE 1 Safety Trades
- **NY PM (Q4):** Manage positions, partial profits

**Session Alignment:**
- **London Open:** Box trades (12/21/22) most effective
- **NY Open:** TYPE 1 + 1H 50-50 Bounce optimal
- **NY PM:** ID 50 Only for late entries
