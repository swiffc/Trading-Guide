üéØ DAY 2 & DAY 3 SPECIFIC SETUPS

Day 2 Setups: Level A1 Sell or Level V1 Buy
üéØ Day 2 Goal: Identify and mark the Peak Formation (PF) that will be traded against on Day 3!

üìä Day 2 Pattern Types:

1. FRD/FGD (First Red/Green Day)

FRD (First Red Day):

  • First significant down day after uptrend
  • Closes below previous day's close
  • Creates bearish PF High
  • Signal: Potential trend reversal beginning

FGD (First Green Day):

  • First significant up day after downtrend
  • Closes above previous day's close
  • Creates bullish PF Low
  • Signal: Potential trend reversal beginning

2. Inside Day

Characteristics:

  • Day 2 range INSIDE Day 1 range
  • High is lower than Day 1 high
  • Low is higher than Day 1 low
  • Consolidation before breakout

Significance:

  • Compression = Explosive move coming
  • Breakout direction = Day 3 direction
  • Often precedes strong Day 3 move

3. False Break Reversal

Pattern:

  • Breaks above/below key level
  • Quickly reverses back inside range
  • Closes opposite to breakout direction
  • Classic "stop hunt" pattern

Trade Setup:

  • False break = PF established
  • Day 3 trade in reversal direction
  • High probability setup

4. Three-Push Pattern

Session Structure:

  • Session 1: Higher high (1st push)
  • Session 2: Higher low (2nd push)
  • Session 3: Three pushes into high, false break, reversal

Identifying:

  • Three distinct pushes in same direction
  • Each push reaches further
  • 3rd push = exhaustion = PF
  • Reversal candle after 3rd push

üîÑ Inside Day Reversal Variation:

Session-by-Session Breakdown:

  1. Session 1 (Asian): 1st push up/down
  2. Session 2 (London): 2nd push (extends Session 1)
  3. Session 3 (NY): 3rd push, break in structure, and a 1-2-3 reversal pattern

What to Watch:

  • If NY session shows 3rd push + reversal = PF confirmed
  • 1-2-3 pattern = New high/low, pullback, continuation opposite
  • This becomes your Day 3 trade reference

üí° Day 2 Key Takeaway: You're NOT trading on Day 2. You're IDENTIFYING which pattern is forming so you know HOW to trade Day 3!

Action: Journal which pattern you see forming. This trains your pattern recognition for future weeks.
