Use this for EVERY trade - No exceptions!

  • What quarter am I in? (Must be Q2 ending or Q3)
  • Where is True Open? (Price positioned correctly vs it?)
  • M or W pattern complete? (Stop hunt occurred?)

üìÖ Daily Trading Routine

Pre-Market (30 min before session)

  • Mark True Day Open (midnight price)
  • Mark True Week Open (Tuesday midnight)
  • Mark True Month Open (if applicable)
  • Mark Weekly PSR Zone (first 8 H1 candles - Sunday 5PM to Monday 1AM EST)
  • Note previous day HOD/LOD on chart
  • Identify Asian range (8pm-12am) - tight or extended?
  • Determine 3-day BTMM cycle position (Day 1, 2, or 3?)
  • Check weekly quarter (Mon=Q1, Tue=Q2, Wed=Q3, Thu=Q4)
  • Set alerts for quarter changes (90-min)
  • Note any major news events today
  • Determine daily bias (above/below True Day Open)

During Trading Session

  • Watch Q1 accumulation (NO TRADES)
  • Identify Q2 manipulation (MARK LEVELS ONLY)
  • Mark the stop hunt high or low
  • Wait for 30-90 min re-accumulation after stop hunt
  • Look for reversal candlestick patterns
  • Enter in Q3 distribution (BEST RR)
  • Set stop loss immediately after entry
  • Set profit targets (TP1, TP2, TP3)
  • Move stop to breakeven after TP1 hit
  • Trail stop after TP2 hit
  • Manage trade through Q4

Post-Market Review (15 min)

  • Journal the trade(s) in detail
  • Did the A-M-D-X cycle play out as expected?
  • Where did the stop hunt occur?
  • Did I enter during Q3 distribution?
  • Was my entry relative to True Open correct?
  • Did I follow my rules?
  • What went well?
  • What could improve?
  • Mark tomorrow's potential PF if visible
  • Prepare analysis for next session
