🛡️ Risk Management

Your Trading Survival Guide

The best strategy in the world is useless if you blow your account. Risk management isn't sexy, but it's what separates winners from losers.

⚠️ CRITICAL TRUTH: 90% of traders fail because of poor risk management, not bad strategies. Master this and you're in the top 10%.

🎯 The Golden Rules

1-2%

Max Risk Per Trade

NEVER risk more than 1-2% of your account on a single trade. No exceptions. Ever.

2-3%

Daily Loss Limit

Stop trading for the day if you lose 2-3%. Walk away. Reset tomorrow.

5-6%

Weekly Loss Limit

Stop trading for the week if you lose 5-6%. Take the week off. Review what went wrong.

MAX 3

Open Positions

Never have more than 3 positions open across all pairs. Focus beats diversification.

📊 Position Sizing Formula

How to Calculate Position Size

Position Size = Risk Amount ÷ (Stop Distance × Pip Value)

Example 1: $10,000 Account

Account Balance: $10,000

Risk per Trade: 1% = $100

Entry: EUR/USD @ 1.0850

Stop Loss: 1.0830 (20 pips)

Pip Value: $10 per pip (standard lot)

Position Size = $100 ÷ (20 × $10)
Position Size = $100 ÷ $200
Position Size = 0.5 standard lots (50,000 units)

Example 2: $5,000 Account

Account Balance: $5,000

Risk per Trade: 2% = $100

Entry: GBP/USD @ 1.2650

Stop Loss: 1.2600 (50 pips)

Pip Value: $10 per pip (standard lot)

Position Size = $100 ÷ (50 × $10)
Position Size = $100 ÷ $500
Position Size = 0.2 standard lots (20,000 units)

⭐ Wednesday 9:00-10:30 AM EST

Weekly Q3 + Daily Q3 + Session Q3 = MAXIMUM EDGE

Action: Trade with full position size, go for TP2/TP3

⭐ Tuesday-Thursday NY Session Q3

9:00-10:30 AM EST daily distribution window

Action: Standard position size, target 1:3 RR

⭐ Day 3 of BTMM Cycle

Whenever distribution occurs (usually Wed/Thu)

Action: Trade against Mon/Tue PF with confidence

⭐ After Clear London Stop Hunt

1:30-4:00 AM manipulation → 9:00 AM NY entry

Action: Enter opposite to London manipulation

⛔ Avoid Trading (Low Probability)

❌ Mondays (Q1 - Accumulation)

Why: Choppy, ranging, unpredictable. Market is accumulating.

Action: Mark PF levels, trade light or not at all.

Risk Reduction: If trading Monday, use 0.5% risk (half size).

❌ Fridays (Special Function)

Why: Unpredictable, profit-taking, balancing. Not part of standard cycle.

Action: Close positions, take profits. Avoid new trades 95% of the time.

Risk Reduction: If setup is crystal clear, use 0.5% risk maximum.

❌ Asian Session (Unless Scalping)

Why: Q1 accumulation, tight ranges, low volume.

Action: Mark Asian range for London/NY reference. Don't trade it.

❌ During Q1 (Any Timeframe)

Why: Accumulation phase, no clear direction.

Action: Wait for Q2 manipulation to complete, then enter in Q3.

❌ Before Stop Hunt Occurs

Why: Entering too early = getting trapped in manipulation.

Action: Be patient! Let them manipulate first, then trade opposite.

🧠 Psychological Risk Management

Mental Game Rules

✅ DO:

  • Follow your rules EVERY time
  • Accept losses as part of the game
  • Journal every single trade
  • Take breaks after 2 losses in a row
  • Celebrate small wins
  • Focus on process, not money
  • Be patient for perfect setups
  • Review trades objectively

❌ DON'T:

  • Revenge trade after a loss
  • Increase size to "make it back"
  • Move stops wider (EVER!)
  • Trade when emotional/tired
  • Chase missed opportunities
  • Trade to hit a profit target
  • Ignore your daily/weekly limits
  • Compare to other traders

📋 Risk Management Checklist

Before Every Trade:

  • Position size calculated (1-2% risk)
  • Stop loss identified and acceptable
  • Risk/reward minimum 1:2 (preferably 1:3+)
  • Not over-leveraged (max 3 open positions)
  • Daily loss limit not reached (2-3% max)
  • Weekly loss limit not reached (5-6% max)
  • Trading during high probability times
  • Not in revenge trade mindset
  • Clear mind and focused

During Trade:

  • Stop loss set and NOT moved wider
  • Profit targets set (TP1, TP2, TP3)
  • Not checking trade every 5 minutes
  • Following scaling out plan

After Trade:

  • Trade journaled with details
  • Lessons learned identified
  • Rules followed or broken noted
  • Not immediately looking for next trade

🚨 Emergency Protocols

If Losing:

  • 🛑 STOP TRADING immediately
  • 📖 Review trade journal
  • 📊 Identify mistake patterns
  • 💰 Reduce position size by 50%
  • 🎯 Return to demo until consistent
  • 🧠 Mental reset - take 3-5 days off

If Winning:

  • ✅ STICK TO THE PLAN
  • 📈 Don't increase size too quickly
  • 🎯 Keep following rules (that's why you're winning!)
  • 💰 Scale up gradually (10% per month max)
  • 🧠 Stay humble - market can change
  • 💵 Consider withdrawing profits
