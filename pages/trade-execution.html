üìà Strategy 1: Large Quarter Transition Trade

Best For: Trending markets, high momentum moves

Win Rate: 60-65% (basic) | 70-75% (with confluence)

Risk/Reward: 2:1 to 3:1

üìã Step-by-Step Execution

1

Look for price approaching a .00, .25, .50, or .75 level

  • Open TradingView with your pair (e.g., EUR/USD)
  • The indicator will show blue dashed lines at LQ Points
  • Example: 1.0750, 1.1000, 1.1250, etc.
2

üìä Check Info Table - BEFORE Price Crosses

Top-right corner of chart - verify current status:

Pre-Entry Checklist:

3

‚ö° Wait for DECISIVE Break

‚úÖ Good Example:

Price moves to: 1.0782 (32 PIPs above) ‚úÖ

This is DECISIVE - shows commitment!

‚ùå Bad Example:

LQ Point: 1.0750

Price moves to: 1.0762 (only 12 PIPs above) ‚ùå

This is NOT decisive - likely a fake breakout!

4

üìà Verify Info Table UPDATED

After the break, Info Table should show:

  • Current LQ: NEW range (e.g., 1.0750 ‚Üí 1.1000)
  • LQ Progress: Low % (10-20%)
  • SQ Completed: 1/10 or 2/10
  • Position: "Hesitation Zone" (yellow box appears)
  • 3-Day Rule: "Day 1/3" ‚úÖ (BEST!)
5

‚è≥ Wait for Pullback

Don't chase! Wait for price to pull back to:

  • LQ Point itself (e.g., back to 1.0750)
  • Within Hesitation Zone (1.0750-1.0775)
  • Look for bullish rejection candle or support confirmation

üí° Pro Tip:

Pullbacks happen 70% of the time after LQ breaks. Be patient!

If no pullback happens and price runs, wait for next setup.

6

üéØ ENTER TRADE

Order Type: Market order or limit order at pullback zone

Direction: LONG

Entry: 1.0755 (pullback to LQ area)

Reason: LQ Transition + Pullback Hold

7

üõë Set Stop Loss

Choose one method:

Conservative Method (Recommended):

Stop = LQ Point - 40 PIPs

Example: 1.0750 - 40 = 1.0710

Risk: 45 PIPs (1.0755 - 1.0710)

Aggressive Method:

Stop = Below Hesitation Zone - 10 PIPs

Example: 1.0750 - 25 - 10 = 1.0715

Risk: 40 PIPs (1.0755 - 1.0715)

8

üéØ Set Profit Targets

Always use 3 targets for scaling out:

Target 1: Half Point (125 PIPs)

T1 = Entry + 125 PIPs

Example: 1.0755 + 125 = 1.0880

Action: Close 30-50% of position

Target 2: Next LQ Point (250 PIPs)

T2 = Next LQ Point

Example: 1.1000

Action: Close 40-60% of remaining

Target 3: Overshoot (275 PIPs)

T3 = LQ Point + 25 PIPs

Example: 1.1000 + 25 = 1.1025

Action: Close final position

9

üí∞ Calculate Position Size

Formula:

Position Size = (Account √ó Risk%) / (Stop PIPs √ó PIP Value)


Example:

Account: $50,000

Risk: 1% = $500

Stop: 45 PIPs

PIP Value: $10/PIP/lot (standard lot)


Position = $500 / (45 √ó $10) = 1.11 lots

Round to: 1.0 lot (conservative)

10

Check Info Table daily:

  • Day 1: Price should reach Hesitation Zone end (LQ + 25 PIPs)
  • Day 2: Price should reach Half Point - Move stop to breakeven!
  • Day 3: Price MUST complete LQ (reach 1.1000) or EXIT

‚ö†Ô∏è Day 3 Warning:

If Info Table shows "Day 3/3" in RED and alert "‚ö†Ô∏è 3-DAY RULE" appears:

‚Üí Take partial profits immediately

‚Üí Tighten stop to recent swing low

‚Üí If not complete by end of day: EXIT TRADE

Execute your scaling plan:

  • At 1.0880 (Half Point): Close 0.3-0.5 lots (30-50%)
  • Move stop to 1.0755 (breakeven)
  • At 1.1000 (LQ Point): Close 0.3-0.4 lots more
  • Move stop to 1.0950 (lock in profits)
  • At 1.1025 (Overshoot): Close remaining 0.1-0.2 lots

‚úÖ Final Result Example:

üí• Strategy 2: Hesitation Zone Breakout

Best For: Continuation moves, after consolidation

Win Rate: 60-65%

Risk/Reward: 2:1

üìã Step-by-Step Execution

1

üëÄ Watch for Hesitation Zone Entry

Price enters new Large Quarter and yellow box appears on chart

  • This is the first 25 PIPs after LQ Point
  • Example: LQ Point 1.0750 ‚Üí HZ is 1.0750-1.0775
  • Info Table shows: Position: "Hesitation Zone"
2

‚è≥ Wait for Consolidation

Price should consolidate 2-8 hours (on 1H chart) or 1-2 days (on 4H chart)

üí° What to Look For:

  • Price bouncing within yellow box
  • Decreasing volatility
  • Smaller candle bodies
  • Multiple tests of HZ boundaries
3

‚ö° Wait for DECISIVE Breakout

Price must break out of HZ by >25 PIPs

‚úÖ Bullish Breakout Example:

HZ Range: 1.0750-1.0775

Breakout: Price moves to 1.0805 (30 PIPs above HZ)

Info Table updates: LQ Progress: 22%, SQ: 2/10

This confirms breakout! ‚úÖ

4

üéØ ENTER on Breakout or Retest

Two entry options:

Option A: Immediate Entry (Aggressive)

Enter as soon as price breaks 25 PIPs past HZ

Example: Enter at 1.0801

Pro: Don't miss the move

Con: Wider stop required

Option B: Retest Entry (Conservative)

Wait for price to retest HZ top as support

Example: Enter at 1.0778 (pullback to HZ)

Pro: Tighter stop

Con: May miss move if no retest

5

üõë Set Stop Loss

Stop Placement:

Stop = Below HZ start - 10 PIPs

Example: 1.0750 - 10 = 1.0740

If entry at 1.0778: Risk = 38 PIPs

If entry at 1.0801: Risk = 61 PIPs

6

üéØ Set Targets

  • Target 1: Half Point (1.0875) - Close 50%
  • Target 2: Next LQ Point (1.1000) - Close remaining
7

üìä Monitor & Manage

  • Check 3-Day Rule status daily
  • Move stop to breakeven when price reaches +50 PIPs
  • Trail stop below each Small Quarter Point (25 PIP intervals)

üéØ Strategy 3: Half Point Bounce

Best For: Range-bound markets, reversals

Win Rate: 65-70% (with confluence)

Risk/Reward: 2:1

üìã Step-by-Step Execution

1

üîç Identify Half Point

Look for purple solid line on chart

  • Half Point = LQ Point + 125 PIPs
  • Example: If LQ is 1.0750-1.1000, Half Point = 1.0875
  • Info Table shows: Position: "Half Point" when nearby
2

üëÄ Watch for Price Approach

Monitor as price approaches Half Point:

  • Check LQ Progress in Info Table: Should be 45-55%
  • Check SQ Completed: Should be 4/10 to 6/10
  • Look for momentum slowing (smaller candles)
3

üìä Look for Rejection Candle

Watch for strong rejection at Half Point:

üí° Rejection Signals:

  • Long Wick: At least 15-20 PIPs above/below Half Point
  • Strong Close: Body closes near opposite end of candle
  • High Volume: If available (stocks/crypto)
  • Confirmation: Next candle continues in rejection direction

‚úÖ Bearish Rejection Example:

Half Point: 1.0875

Candle: Open 1.0872, High 1.0884, Low 1.0865, Close 1.0867

Analysis: 9 PIP wick above Half Point, closed near low

Next candle: Closes at 1.0858 (confirms rejection)

Action: ENTER SHORT at 1.0858 ‚úÖ

4

üî• Check Confluence (Important!)

Half Point bounces work BEST with high confluence:

Confluence Checklist:

‚ö†Ô∏è Low Confluence Warning:

If confluence <3 points: SKIP THIS TRADE

Half Point bounces without confluence have only ~50% win rate (coin flip)

5

üéØ ENTER on Confirmation Candle

Entry Rules:

Wait for confirmation candle to close

Enter at close of confirmation candle

Or enter on next candle open


Example:

Rejection candle closes: 1.0867

Confirmation candle closes: 1.0858 ‚úÖ

ENTER SHORT at 1.0858

6

üõë Set Stop Loss

Stop Placement:

For SHORT: Stop = Half Point + 20 PIPs

Example: 1.0875 + 20 = 1.0895

Risk: 37 PIPs (1.0895 - 1.0858)


For LONG: Stop = Half Point - 20 PIPs

7

üéØ Set Targets

For Bounces BACK to LQ Point:

Target 1: 50% retracement

Example: (1.0875 - 1.0750) / 2 = 1.0812

Target 2: Return to LQ Point

Example: 1.0750

Close remaining 50%


Total Potential: 125 PIPs (full retracement)

üìã Step-by-Step Execution

  • OVERSHOOT: Price exceeds LQ Point by <25 PIPs
  • UNDERSHOOT: Price falls short of LQ Point by <25 PIPs

Check Position row - should show "LQ Point"

üí° Overshoot/Undershoot Meaning:

OVERSHOOT (Bearish Signal):

  • Price tried to break above LQ Point
  • Failed to move >25 PIPs
  • Shows exhaustion/weakness
  • Likely to reverse back down

UNDERSHOOT (Bullish Signal):

  • Price tried to reach LQ Point
  • Came within 25 PIPs but failed
  • Shows support holding
  • Likely to reverse back up
3

üëÄ Wait for Rejection Confirmation

Don't enter immediately - wait for confirmation:
