Pre-Market Mindset

Before the session

  • Review yesterday's trades
  • Check economic calendar
  • Identify today's Q phase
  • Set daily risk limit (2-4%)
  • Plan A+ setups only
  • Meditation: 5 minutes
üéØ

During-Trade Mindset

When positions are open

  • Trust your stop loss
  • Don't move SL wider
  • Scale out at TP1/TP2/TP3
  • Let winners run
  • No emotional reactions
  • Breathe: Stay present

After-Trade Mindset

When session closes

  • Win or lose: What learned?
  • Did I follow my plan?
  • No revenge trading
  • Review confluence score
  • Rest: Tomorrow is new

üö® Circuit Breakers - When to STOP Trading

üõë

Daily Stop Rules

  • 2 losses in a row: Stop for the day
  • 3% account loss: Stop for the day
  • Emotional state: Angry, fearful, or euphoric? Stop.
  • No A+ setups: Don't force trades. Walk away.
‚è∏Ô∏è

Weekly/Monthly Stop Rules

  • Down 3 weeks straight: Take 1 week off. Review system.
  • 10% monthly loss: Stop trading for 2 weeks. Reassess.
  • Overconfidence: 3+ wins? Stay humble. Stick to plan.
  • Life stress: Personal issues? No trading until resolved.
