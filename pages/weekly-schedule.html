üìÜ Day-by-Day Breakdown

üåÖ Monday - Q1 Accumulation

Characteristics:

  • Market sets the range for the week
  • Choppy, ranging price action
  • Peak Formation (PF) often established
  • Volume builds slowly
  • Retail traders establish positions

Strategy:

  • PRIMARY: Mark PF High/Low on charts
  • OBSERVE: Which direction is being pushed?
  • IDENTIFY: Weekly PSR zone (first 8 H1 candles)
  • TRADE LIGHT: Only crystal clear setups
  • JOURNAL: Note potential PF for reversal
‚ö†Ô∏è Monday Warning: Most traders lose on Mondays. Sit out or trade minimal size. Your job is to OBSERVE, not profit.
üå§Ô∏è Tuesday - Q2 Manipulation

Characteristics:

  • True Week Open at Tuesday 12:00am
  • Continuation of Monday's move
  • More volume trapped on wrong side
  • Often extends Monday's PF
  • Market makers accumulating against retail

Strategy:

  • MARK: True Week Open (Tuesday midnight)
  • WATCH: Does price extend Monday's move?
  • CONFIRM: PF High or PF Low solidifying?
  • PREPARE: Setup alerts for Wednesday
  • POSITION: Small positions if clear setup
üí° Tuesday Tip: If Monday made high and Tuesday extends it = PF High confirmed. Expect Wednesday reversal down. Vice versa for lows.
‚òÄÔ∏è Wednesday - Q3 Distribution üî•üî•üî•

Characteristics:

  • BEST DAY of the week to trade
  • Real move begins against Monday/Tuesday
  • BTMM Day 3 = Distribution phase
  • Clearest trends and setups
  • Extended ranges (100-200+ pips)
  • Retail stops triggered = liquidity

Strategy:

  • TRADE: Against Monday/Tuesday PF
  • ENTER: NY Session Q3 (9:00-10:30 AM EST) üî•
  • SIZE UP: Use full position size
  • TARGETS: Go for TP2 and TP3
  • HOLD: Let winners run into Thursday
  • CONFIRMATION: Look for 50/800 or 200/800 EMA cross = Level III!
üéØ Wednesday Power Move: This is YOUR DAY! Weekly Q3 + BTMM Day 3 + Level III + Daily Q3 alignment = Maximum edge. Trade with confidence!

üåÜ Thursday - Q4 Reversal/Continuation

Characteristics:

  • Market decides direction for next week
  • Either continues Wednesday move
  • Or reverses to reset for Friday
  • Pullbacks in established trends
  • Often consolidates gains

Strategy:

  • MANAGE: Wednesday positions
  • TRADE: Pullbacks in trend direction
  • SCALE OUT: Take partial profits
  • TRAIL: Move stops to protect gains
  • PREPARE: For Friday close
üí° Thursday Strategy: If Wednesday distributed up, look for pullback longs on Thursday. Vice versa for downtrends.
üåô Friday - Special Function

Characteristics:

  • NOT part of standard weekly quarters
  • Market makers "balance the books"
  • Often returns toward weekly open
  • Lower volume, erratic movement
  • Profit-taking dominates
  • Sets up for next week

Strategy:

  • CLOSE: Most or all positions
  • TAKE PROFITS: Lock in the week's gains
  • AVOID: New trades (95% of the time)
  • REVIEW: Week's performance
  • PLAN: For next week's cycle
‚õî Friday Warning: Friday is UNPREDICTABLE. Protect your profits! Only trade if setup is absolutely crystal clear. When in doubt, sit out.

üìà Weekly Trading Plan Template

Your Weekly Routine

Sunday Evening Prep:

  • Review previous week's trades
  • Calculate weekly win rate and RR
  • Mark major weekly levels on charts
  • Check economic calendar for news
  • Set mindset for patient Monday observation

Monday:

  • Mark PF High and Low as they form
  • Identify Weekly PSR zone (first 8 H1 candles)
  • Note which direction is being pushed
  • Trade LIGHT or not at all
  • Journal potential reversal zones

Tuesday:

  • Mark True Week Open (Tuesday 12:00am)
  • Confirm if Monday PF is holding
  • Watch for extension of Monday's move
  • Set alerts for Wednesday NY session
  • Small position if clear setup appears

Wednesday:

  • Wake up early - this is YOUR day!
  • Enter trades against Mon/Tue PF
  • Use full position size (1-2% risk)
  • Target TP2 and TP3
  • Let winners run - don't exit early!

Thursday:

  • Manage Wednesday positions
  • Take partial profits (TP1, TP2)
  • Trail stops on remaining position
  • Look for pullback entries in trend
  • Prepare for Friday close

Friday:

  • Close or heavily reduce positions
  • Take profits - lock in the week
  • Avoid new trades (unless crystal clear)
  • Journal the full week
  • Celebrate wins, learn from losses
